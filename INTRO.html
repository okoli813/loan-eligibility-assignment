BETA LOAN

This is a loan application, that grants you loan with low interest rates

If you want to request for a loan Please clearly read the notes below to know if you are eligible to take a loan

A loan will be given if you have the needed requirements

  • 1. The current amount in your Account must be more than the loan amount required, If your current balance is more than the loan amount you will be awarded 10 points, but if less, you will be deducted 10 points
  • 2. if you have a credit history of six months,you will be given 10 points,else,0 points
  • 3. if your Last deposit date is Within a month you will be given 5 points,else,0 points
  • 4. if your Last Loan collection date is above 6 months you will be given 10 points,else,0 points
  • 5. if Loan repayment Period was Below 6 months you will be given 5 points, else, 0 points
  • 6. if you have a savings account you will be given 5 points, else,0 points
  • 7. if you have a current account you will be given 10 points, else,0 points

    • Note

    Loan can only be awarded if your score is a above 30 points
